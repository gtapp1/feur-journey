FEU-Roosevelt Freshmen Journey

"Our Journey as College Freshmen" is a heartfelt digital scrapbook capturing our unforgettable experiences at FEU Roosevelt. From creative Halloween costumes to joyful Pre-Christmas celebrations, this album showcases how we navigated the ups and downs of our freshman year. It reflects our growth, friendships, and the life lessons we've learned along the way.
